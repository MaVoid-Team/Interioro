# Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Creative North Star: "The Digital Greenhouse"
The brand is not just an e-commerce store; it is a curated, lived-in, organic environment. The goal is to feel "grown," not "built."

## Tone Characteristics
- **Artistic & Authoritative:** Use Noto Serif for headlines to project elegant, artistic authority.
- **Modern & Legible:** Use Manrope for functional data to ensure clarity and stability.
- **Warm & Cozy:** Avoid pure black. Use `on-surface` (#231a0a) to maintain a welcoming atmosphere.
- **Editorial:** The layout and copy should mimic a high-end magazine or a physical art gallery.

## Copywriting Principles
- **Curated Language:** Instead of "Buy Now" or "Shop," use language that suggests curation and discovery.
- **Organic Flow:** Content should feel layered and intentional, avoiding rigid, boxy structures in both visual and textual presentation.

## Special Components
- **The Curator's Note:** Use this for interior design tips. The tone should be helpful, expert, and slightly personal, as if a curator is speaking directly to the customer.
